ABOUT


Hi, I’m Matthew J. Moses! I’m from Metuchen, New Jersey, and I am the founder of MR.GRINDTIME TRUCKING,LLC. I started MR.GRINDTIME TRUCKING,LLC because I have a strong passion for driving trucks and wanted to inspire others to start driving, too. After many years of trying to find a job that reflected my love for driving, I decided to take matters into my own hands and start my own business. MR.GRINDTIME TRUCKING,LLC is an all-in-one driving platform that can help fellow aspiring drivers learn their own way one truck at a time. Since I started MR.GRINDTIME TRUCKING,LLC in 2022, it’s quickly become one of the most popular trucking platforms online, with more than 5,000 subscribers nationwide. I’ve been in your shoes before, so here at MR.GRINDTIME TRUCKING,LLC my goal is to help you achieve your trucking/owner/operator dreams, whatever they may be.
